Who are we?

The 332nd Legion is a rapidly-expanding StarSim community that recruits Star Wars and Arma fans to the front lines of the Clone Wars to see what it's like to be a clone firsthand. The founding of the 332nd Unit was established to become a military styled Arma 3 unit. A sanctuary for those looking for an authentic Star Wars experience. We as a community aim to provide a welcoming atmosphere in which you can make new friends and welcome old ones, and most importantly have fun and shoot clankers. The 332nd hopes offer a fun, yet semi-serious environment. We currently have our own aux team making new updates regularly with new textured armor and vehicles keeping the gameplay refreshing. Our goal is to allow anyone to pursue any particular role they want to their fullest; whether that be a regular trooper who can advance through the specialist ranks or a pilot fighting for air superiority, as well as much more! The 332nd's main goal is to create a fun place for its members to play a video game in a star wars environment. At all times our goal is to give its members a structured playing style with CoC and light training to develop its community into something more.
