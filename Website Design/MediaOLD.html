Machinima Project

The Machinima Project Team (MPT) is in charge of creating a 332nd stlye machinima series. To put it simply, the word machinima is a method of making animated film using software similar to the design of video games or literally using video games as the film. We, as the MPT, want to create a series using the 332nd Arma unit, creating our own; story, lore, and anything needed for our project. Currently, the team is working on season one of the show. You can watch it as it comes out on our YouTube Channel https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC-UZ_I5ukHsoCbORnFVo-QA.
