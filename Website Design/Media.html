Media Team

The media team is in charge of all the media accounts the legion owns. This includes, but is not limited to, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube. They are in charge of posting content and general management of these items and any media items added under the media team in the future. Links for all of our media sites can be found at the bottom of the page.
