Interested in joining the 332nd?

All rules listed below are the standard and rules of the 332nd Legion and will be followed accordingly

  • Bullying, disrespect, harassment, or anything with ill intent is strictly prohibited and will be handled accordingly which may result in a dishonorable discharge/unit ban
  • Trolling, spam or harm to anyone in our Teamspeak & discord is prohibited.
  • Exploiting any glitches, hacks, or client-side mods that give any member an advantage over others is strictly prohibited.
  • Poaching and advertising to other members of other starsim is strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate unit ban.
  • Any abuse of C-shop permission is strictly prohibited and will be handled accordingly in each case.
  • All members will respect the rank of other unit members and the overall CoC.
  • All members regardless of rank will be held to the same standard from Cadets to Lieutenants and will be met with the same punishment regardless of rank.
  • The Gear & Uniform Guide will be abided by and enforced again by all members from Cadet to Lieutenants.
  • Antagonizing other members to break unit rules is also the same as violating the rules and will be met with the same punishment.
  • Multi-Starsiming is allowed, please refer to Pg.12, as well as other milsim units are allowed as long as times don't conflict.
  • Drinking is allowed on servers/Teamspeak (within moderation) as long as the individual is of proper age and abided by unit rules.
  • Friends of the Unit members are allowed to join in any of the unit activities they are just required to wear plain trooper gear.
  • Anything related to the 332nd Starsim must be in official channels meaning no private discords regarding the unit.

Unit Guest Rules

  • All Guests must follow Unit rules for Discord, TeamSpeak, our Servers, and any other event-related regulations. These rules can be found in our constitution and you are required to review and follow them.
  • Guests must join the discord and request tags before showing up on TeamSpeak. This allows us to track who joined when.
  • Anyone who is in another unit must format their name to include their unit in our discord.
  • Guests that are not in another unit must mention which member(s) they are friends with (Host Friend). Guests who are not in another unit may NOT play in our main ops, skirmishes, or fun ops. They can participate in our TeamSpeak, Minecraft Server, and other Events that are not Arma 3 related.
  • If the Host Friend leaves our unit, a guest can stay as a guest so long as they get approval from the Company Adjutant. The Guest is responsible for reaching out to get this approval, otherwise, their role will be removed after 2 weeks.
  • All Friends of the Unit (FoU) must go through an approval process before getting their role that includes getting approval from the Company Adjutant and their Unit's Leader.
