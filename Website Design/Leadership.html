Leadership

The Company Staff is the highest level of command in the legion. They oversee all the platoons we have in the 332nd.


Company Commander: CC Riddle

The Company Commander's role is to run the Company from an admin perspective i.e helping to write and put in place new policies & rules within the Company. As well as handling any issues that could arise.

Company Executive Officer: CC Poseidon

The Company Executive Officer's role is to assist the Company Commander to run the Company from an admin perspective i.e helping to write and put in place new policies & rules within the Company. As well as handling any issues that could arise.

Company Advisor: CC Falx

The Company Advisor is a Company role that focuses on the well-being of ALL individuals within the Company. This individual is the third IC of the Company and should be able to run everything if the Company Executive Officer & Commander are absent. Additionally, this individual should strive in conflict issues and assist with any sort of disciplinaries. Is an Officer Billet.

Company Adjutant: CC Rev

There are a lot of Starsim units out there. A whole lot. The Company Adjutant's primary job is to build connections with other units. They act as an ambassador of sorts. They are also in charge of our outreach discord, Little Kamino. Outreach like this allows us to share knowledge with other communities, plan larger events like joint ops and charity events, and will allow leaders of other units to have a source of contact for our unit. The Company Adjutant is a major face of the unit. You have to be calm, collected, and dedicated to holding this position.
